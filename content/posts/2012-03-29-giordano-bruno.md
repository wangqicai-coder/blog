## 火刑堆上的哲学家

**Giordano Bruno（1548-1600）**

**February 17, 1600——**

**Campo de' Fiori广场，Rome**

**被绑在stake上——**

**舌头locked（防止他speak）——**

**活活burned**

**罪名：heresy**

**他的"crimes"：**

- 否认transubstantiation（圣餐变体）
- 质疑Virgin Birth
- 主张宇宙infinite
- 相信countless worlds
- 泛神论tendencies

**他拒绝recant——**

**Facing inquisitors说：**

> "Perhaps you who pronounce my sentence are in greater fear than I who receive it."

**死时：**

**Silent（因为tongue locked）——**

**但eyes据说burning with defiance**

**Today，在那广场——**

**有他的statue**

**Symbol of：**

**Thought的freedom——**

**对抗dogma**

## 生平：从修士到异端

### 早年

**出生于Nola（near Naples）**

**故sometimes called "the Nolan"**

**1565（17岁）：加入Dominican order**

**Take name Giordano（俗名Filippo）**

**在Naples修道院——**

**Study Aristotle、Aquinas**

**但also secret读：**

- Hermetic texts
- Neoplatonism
- Nicholas of Cusa
- Copernicus（newly published）

**很快有trouble：**

**被怀疑heterodox views**

**Remove pictures of saints from cell——**

**只留crucifix**

### Wandering years

**1576：Leave order（flee，actually）**

**Under suspicion of heresy**

**开始nearly 20年wandering：**

**Geneva（但clash with Calvinists）**

**France（Paris、Toulouse）——**

**Teach、debate、write**

**England（1583-1585）——**

**Most productive period**

**Stay with French ambassador**

**Visit Oxford（disaster——被mock）**

**Write major cosmological works**

**Germany（Frankfurt、其他）**

**Prague**

**不断moving——**

**Always controversy跟随**

### Fatal return

**1591：**

**Invitation from Venetian nobleman Giovanni Mocenigo——**

**Teach memory arts**

**Bruno accept（需要钱）——**

**Return to Italy**

**Mocenigo后来denounce Bruno to Inquisition——**

**（可能因为disappointed in teachings）**

**1592：Arrested in Venice**

**1593：Transferred to Rome（worse）**

**7年imprisonment、interrogation**

**1600：Final sentence——**

**Death by burning**

## 无限宇宙的Vision

**Bruno最radical idea：**

### Copernicus的问题

**Copernicus（1543）propose：**

**Sun（不是Earth）=center of universe**

**Revolutionary！**

**但Copernicus仍assume：**

**Universe=finite**

**Sphere of fixed stars**

**Only one solar system**

**Bruno：这不够radical！**

### Infinite universe

**Bruno宣称：**

**Universe=infinite in extent**

**无边界、无中心**

**无数stars**

**每个star=sun**

**每个sun有planets**

**每个planet可能inhabited！**

**Countless worlds**

**这是astonishing leap——**

**From Copernicus's heliocentric universe——**

**To infinite、pluralistic cosmos**

### 哪里来这idea？

**不是from observation（no telescope yet）**

**而是philosophical reasoning：**

**1. 上帝's infinite power**

**Infinite God应该create infinite universe——**

**才配他的greatness**

**Finite universe=limitation on上帝**

**（This from Nicholas of Cusa某程度）**

**2. No reason for limit**

**为什么universe stop at某点？**

**空间beyond？**

**不合理**

**如果infinite space——**

**为什么only one solar system in infinite space？**

**3. 丰饶原则（Principle of Plenitude）**

**上帝's creativity should fill all possibilities**

**Infinite worlds=manifestation of divine abundance**

### Implications

**这vision=revolutionary：**

**Earth不是中心（Copernicus已说）**

**甚至Sun不是中心（Bruno add）**

**没有center——**

**或everywhere=center**

**Humanity不是special**

**Countless other beings可能存在**

**上帝's creation=vastly larger than imagined**

**这挑战：**

- Aristotelian cosmos
- Biblical geography（heaven above, hell below）
- Human特殊地位

## 泛神论与上帝观

**Bruno的theology=heterodox：**

### God和Nature

**上帝=不只是transcendent Creator**

**而是immanent in nature**

**Nature=上帝's body某种意义**

**上帝和universe不是two separate things——**

**而是one**

**这是pantheism（泛神论）**

**Or panentheism（万有在神论）？**

**Bruno不总是clear**

**但definitely非orthodox**

### Anima Mundi（世界灵魂）

**Universe animated by universal soul**

**一切=alive（某种意义）**

**不只是inert matter**

**Vitalism**

**Stars、planets、atoms=all有life principle**

### Unity和multiplicity

**从Nicholas of Cusa：**

**Coincidence of opposites**

**上帝=both unity和multiplicity**

**One expressed in infinite many**

**Many=manifestations of One**

**Neoplatonic emanation——**

**但more pantheistic**

### Magic和Nature

**Understanding nature's secret powers=**

**Accessing divine**

**Natural magic（不是demonic）**

**通过knowledge of correspondences、sympathies**

**Hermetic tradition**

**这was suspect to Church——**

**Border between natural magic和sorcery=blurry**

## 记忆术（Art of Memory）

**Bruno也famous for：**

### Classical tradition

**Ancient Greeks（Simonides）develop memory techniques：**

**Mental"places"存store information**

**Renaissance revival（via medieval texts）**

**Bruno master和innovator**

### Hermetic memory

**Bruno用memory theater不只是store facts——**

**而是：**

**Create mental cosmos**

**通过magical symbols、correspondences**

**Ordering mind=ordering universe**

**Microcosm mirrors macrocosm**

**Memory成为spiritual、magical practice**

**这是why Mocenigo想learn from Bruno——**

**Expect practical memory technique**

**但Bruno teach metaphysical system**

**Disappointing！**

## Philosophy的其他方面

### Coincidence of opposites

**从Nicholas of Cusa：**

**在infinite，contraries unite**

**Minimum和maximum=same**

**Circle with infinite radius=straight line**

**Bruno extend这到ethics、theology**

### 变化与恒定

**一切在flux（Heraclitus）**

**但underlying unity（Parmenides）**

**Both true**

### Atoms

**Revival of atomism（Democritus、Lucretius）**

**最小particles**

**Combine into all things**

**但Bruno的atoms=alive（vs purely mechanical）**

## Bruno vs Contemporaries

### vs Aristotelians

**Reject finite cosmos**

**Reject elements理论**

**Reject teleology（certain aspects）**

### vs Church

**Obviously conflict**

**Orthodoxy require：**

- Finite universe
- Earth special（redemption发生这里）
- Literal reading of certain Scripture

**Bruno challenge all**

### vs Galileo（稍后）

**Interesting对比：**

**两者都support Copernicus**

**但：**

**Bruno=philosophical、speculative**

**Galileo=empirical、observational**

**Bruno因infinite universe被burn**

**Galileo（后来）因heliocentrism被condemn——**

**但submit、recant、survive（house arrest）**

**Different temperaments**

## 为什么被Burn？

**具体charges：**

### 1. Theological heresies

**否认Trinity（某种意义）**

**否认transubstantiation**

**质疑Virgin Birth、Christ's divinity（certain aspects）**

**这是core doctrines**

### 2. 宇宙学

**Infinite worlds**

**No special place for Earth**

**这挑战Biblical cosmology**

### 3. 泛神论

**上帝和nature合一？**

**Deny transcendent God？**

**Heretical**

### 4. Magic

**Hermetic practices**

**Suspicious of sorcery**

### 5. Obstinacy

**Refuse recant**

**Inquisition给机会——**

**多次**

**他拒绝**

**这seal fate**

**如果他recant（like Galileo later）——**

**Might survive**

**但Bruno：**

**Principle over survival**

## Legacy

### Martyr for science？

**19-20世纪：**

**Bruno celebrated as martyr for scientific freedom**

**Against religious obscurantism**

**Statue in Rome（1889）——**

**Controversy（Church oppose）**

**Symbol of secularism、free thought**

### But complex

**Bruno不是scientist（modern sense）**

**不做experiments、observations**

**He的infinite universe=philosophical speculation——**

**Not empirical**

**He的reasons混合：**

- Neoplatonism
- Hermeticism
- Magic
- 某些sound reasoning

**现代科学会reject many他的ideas——**

**（Living stars、world soul、magic）**

**但accept infinite universe、many worlds**

**所以：**

**Partly right for partly wrong reasons？**

### Influence on philosophy

**Leibniz读他**

**Spinoza的pantheism可能influenced？**

**Schelling、German Romantics**

**Process philosophy某些ideas**

## 批评

### 1. Reckless？

**Return to Italy=foolish**

**Knew danger**

**Could have stayed safer地方**

**Stubbornness cost life**

### 2. Confused？

**Mix philosophy、magic、theology unclearly**

**不是rigorous system**

**Certain contradictions**

### 3. Arrogant？

**Contemptuous of opponents**

**Combative**

**Made unnecessary enemies**

### 4. Hermeticism misguided

**基于forged texts（though他不知道）**

**Later scholarship show Hermetic corpus=late antique forgery——**

**Not ancient Egyptian wisdom**

**Bruno的foundation=shaky**

## 现代意义

### 1. 思想自由

**Bruno代表：**

**Right to think、speak freely**

**Even heterodox ideas**

**Martyrdom for intellectual freedom**

**今天仍relevant**

### 2. 宇宙的无限性

**现代cosmology confirm：**

**Universe vastly larger than Renaissance imagined**

**Possibly infinite**

**Billions of galaxies**

**Exoplanets everywhere**

**Bruno's vision=vindicated（某种意义）**

**虽然he的reasoning不同**

### 3. 人类的位置

**Copernican revolution**

**Darwinian revolution**

**一系列"decentering"**

**人类不是cosmic center**

**Bruno foresee这**

### 4. Limits of authority

**Who决定truth？**

**Church？State？**

**Or individual reason、inquiry？**

**Bruno代表：**

**Truth不是decreed——**

**而是discovered**

## 实践思考

### 1. Courage of conviction

**Bruno refuse compromise principle**

**这admirable——**

**但also：**

**When是stubbornness，when是integrity？**

**Difficult line**

### 2. Wonder at cosmos

**Bruno's awe at infinite universe**

**今天we know even more vastness**

**Maintain that wonder**

### 3. Question orthodoxy

**但also：**

**不是all heterodoxy=true**

**Bruno wrong about很多**

**Critical thinking，not just rebellion**

### 4. 整合知识？

**Bruno try synthesize everything**

**Admirable impulse——**

**但method problematic**

**How integrate without confusion？**
